Flowave — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Flowave does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app requires no account and includes no analytics, advertising, or tracking.

Focus / Pomodoro history

If you enable iCloud in Settings, your Pomodoro history and current session are stored in your personal iCloud account (Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage) so they can sync across your devices. This data is controlled by you through your Apple ID; the developer has no access to it. If iCloud is disabled, this data stays only on your device.

Notifications

Flowave uses local notifications to alert you to Pomodoro phase changes. No notification data leaves your device.

Audio

All sound is generated on your device in real time. Flowave does not require a network connection to play audio and does not transmit audio data.

Children

Flowave is rated 4+ and collects no data, so it is suitable for all ages.
