Flowave — Support

Focus, relax, and sleep with binaural beats, generative music, and nature sounds — plus a built-in Pomodoro timer.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need headphones?
Headphones or earbuds are recommended for binaural beats — the effect relies on hearing a slightly different frequency in each ear. Ambient sounds and the modular synth tracks work fine on speakers.
Does it work offline?
Yes. All audio is generated on your device in real time. No account or internet connection is required.
How does iCloud sync work?
When iCloud is enabled in Settings, your Pomodoro history and current session sync across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac. You can start a session on one device and continue on another. Turn iCloud off to keep everything only on the current device.
Can I mix sounds?
Yes. Layer any binaural beat or synth track with one or more ambient sounds, each with its own volume.
Is Flowave a medical product?
No. Flowave is a relaxation, focus, and productivity tool and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition.
